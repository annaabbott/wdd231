Project Site Plan

Site Name: Una Obra Maravillosa

The projected auience for this webpage is returned missionaries who served missions in the Spain Las Palmas mission. This audience already has a Facebook page, called Una Obra Maravillosa. I chose to keep the same name for the webpage so that it would be immediately recognizable to the Facebook audience.

Site Purpose:

To provide information about current and former missionaries serving in the Spanish Canary Islands, as well as members of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints who live there.

User Scenarios:

As a former missionary who served in Tenerife, I want to find information about upcoming mission reunions.

As a son/daughter of a woman who served in Gran Canaria, I want to find pictures from my mother's mission.
